# Requirements

## Example Scenario

You have recently been hired to work in the healthcare field. Your new manager has asked you to write a blog post describing a health-related product, practice, or process that at least one group of employees will soon implement.

---

## Task Requirements

> Your submission must represent your original work and understanding of the course material.

### A. Explainer Blog Post

Create an explainer blog post that discusses a health-related product, practice, or process that your organization will soon implement. The blog post should be a **maximum of 1,000 words** and must include:

1. A thorough explanation of your chosen health-related product, practice, or process based on your research from **three distinct sources**.
2. A compelling argument of how the health-related product, practice, or process will benefit the employees or organization when it is implemented.

### B. Audience

Identify and define the employee group (the audience) for the blog post.

1. Explain how this audience influenced your choices for the document's etiquette, including word choice and tone.

### C. Sources

Acknowledge sources, using in-text citations and references, for content that is quoted, paraphrased, or summarized.

### D. Professional Communication

Demonstrate professional communication in the content and presentation of your submission.

---

## Example Topics

- Implementing a mindfulness and stress reduction program for nursing staff
- Adopting telehealth platforms for patient follow-up visits
- Rolling out electronic health record (EHR) interoperability standards for clinical teams
- Introducing wearable health monitoring devices for frontline healthcare workers
- Deploying AI-assisted diagnostic tools for radiology departments
