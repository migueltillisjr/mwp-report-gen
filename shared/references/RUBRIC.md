# Rubric

| Section | Not Evident | Approaching Competence | Competent |
|---------|-------------|------------------------|-----------|
| **A. Blog Post** | An explainer blog post is not provided. | The explainer blog post is more than 1,000 words, or it does not discuss a health-related product, practice, or process for organizational implementation. | The explainer blog post is not more than 1,000 words and discusses a health-related product, practice, or process for organizational implementation. |
| **A1. Explanation** | The blog post does not provide an explanation of the chosen health-related product, practice, or process. | The blog post does not accurately explain the chosen health-related product, practice, or process or does not provide 3 research sources. | The blog post accurately explains the chosen health-related product, practice, or process and provides 3 research sources as the basis for that explanation. |
| **A2. Compelling Argument** | The blog post does not provide an argument of how the health-related product, practice, or process could benefit the employees or organization. | The blog post provides an argument of how the health-related product, practice, or process could benefit the employees or organization that is not compelling or is irrelevant. | The blog post provides a compelling argument of how the health-related product, practice, or process could benefit the employees or organization. |
| **B. Employee Group** | An employee group is not identified and defined. | The employee group is identified, but the group is either not defined or is not appropriate for the chosen health-related product, practice, or process. | The employee group is identified and defined and is appropriate for the chosen health-related product, practice, or process. |
| **B1. Analyzing Business Etiquette** | An explanation of how the audience for the blog post affected the document's word choice and tone is not provided. | The explanation is missing either how the audience affected word choice or how the audience affects tone. | The explanation accurately discusses how the audience for the blog post affected the document's word choice and tone. |
| **C. Sources** | The submission does not include both in-text citations and a reference list for sources that are quoted, paraphrased, or summarized. | The submission includes in-text citations for sources that are quoted, paraphrased, or summarized and a reference list; however, the citations or reference list is incomplete or inaccurate. | The submission includes in-text citations for sources that are properly quoted, paraphrased, or summarized and a reference list that accurately identifies the author, date, title, and source location as available. |
| **D. Professional Communication** | This submission includes pervasive errors in professional communication related to grammar, sentence fluency, contextual spelling, or punctuation, negatively impacting the professional quality and clarity of the writing. | This submission includes substantial errors in professional communication related to grammar, sentence fluency, contextual spelling, or punctuation. | This submission includes satisfactory use of grammar, sentence fluency, contextual spelling, and punctuation, which promote accurate interpretation and understanding. |
